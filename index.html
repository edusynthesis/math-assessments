About EDUsynthesis

Built by a tutor who lives in these transitions.

EDUsynthesis is led by Olusegun Ilori, a mathematics tutor with over 20 years of experience and a Global Math Project Ambassador. He works daily with students moving between grades and between curricula — Edexcel, Cambridge, Ontario, Nigerian Basic Education, US and Dubai/UAE frameworks among them — which is exactly the gap these diagnostics are built to close.
